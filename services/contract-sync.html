Keynote Advisory provides specialized contract negotiation services and sync licensing representation for artists, songwriters, producers, and labels. We secure favorable terms in recording agreements, publishing deals, collaboration contracts, and licensing opportunities while placing your music in film, television, advertising, and gaming projects that generate income and exposure.

Contract Negotiation

Recording Agreements

Expert representation in negotiations with record labels, distributors, and production companies. We ensure favorable terms on royalty rates and calculation methods, advance structures and recoupment terms, creative control provisions, rights retention and reversion clauses, territory and term limitations, marketing commitments, and option periods and release requirements.

Publishing Deals

Negotiation of publishing agreements including administration deals, co-publishing arrangements, work-for-hire agreements, sub-publishing contracts, and catalog acquisition/sale transactions. We ensure you maintain appropriate ownership, receive competitive advances, and secure favorable royalty splits.

Collaboration Agreements

Clear, enforceable contracts for producer agreements, featured artist appearances, songwriter splits, sample clearances, remix rights, and work-for-hire arrangements. Proper collaboration agreements prevent disputes and ensure everyone receives appropriate credit and compensation.

"Protect your value. Maximize your leverage. Secure your future."

Sync Licensing

Placement Opportunities

We actively pitch your catalog to music supervisors, advertising agencies, production companies, and game developers for placement in film & television productions, advertising campaigns, video games, streaming series, corporate videos, and trailers & promos.

License Negotiation

When placement opportunities arise, we negotiate terms including license fees (upfront sync fees), usage parameters (media, territory, term), rights granted (master, composition, both), backend royalty provisions, credit requirements, and most favored nations clauses.

Catalog Representation

We maintain your catalog in professional pitch format with organized metadata and search tags, clean master and publishing rights, instrumental and alternate versions, competitive pricing structure, and quick clearance capabilities.
