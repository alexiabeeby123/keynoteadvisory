Core Services

Copyright Registration & Protection

Comprehensive registration of your musical compositions with relevant copyright offices, performing rights organizations, and mechanical rights societies. We establish clear ownership, protect your intellectual property, and create the foundation for royalty collection.

Mechanical Royalty Collection

Collection of mechanical royalties generated by physical sales, digital downloads, and interactive streaming across all platforms and territories. We ensure every reproduction of your composition generates the income you're entitled to.

Performance Royalty Administration

Management of performance royalties from radio play, television broadcasts, live venues, streaming services, and public performance. We work with PROs globally to capture income from every public performance of your work.

Catalog Administration

Ongoing management of your entire publishing catalog including registration maintenance, metadata accuracy, ownership tracking, and dispute resolution. We keep your catalog organized and optimized for maximum income.

Why Publishing Matters

Many songwriters leave significant money uncollected because publishing is complex and requires specialized administration. The difference between managed and unmanaged publishing can mean hundreds of thousands in lost lifetime revenue.

Multiple Income Streams

A single composition can generate income from mechanical royalties (physical sales, downloads, streams), performance royalties (radio, TV, live performance, streaming), synchronization fees (film, TV, advertising, gaming), print royalties (sheet music), and foreign royalties (international territories).

Global Collection Network

We maintain relationships with collecting societies in over 100 territories, ensuring your international income reaches you. Without proper administration, foreign royalties often go uncollected.
