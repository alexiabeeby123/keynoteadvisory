Advanced Services

Metadata Optimization

Proper metadata is critical for discoverability, playlist consideration, and accurate royalty payment. We ensure complete and accurate artist, track, and album information; genre and mood tags optimized for algorithmic recommendations; credits and contributor information; language and territory-specific metadata; and cover art meeting platform specifications.

ISRC & UPC Management

Unique identifier codes that track your music's usage: ISRC (International Standard Recording Code) for each track, UPC (Universal Product Code) for each release, proper code registration and database linkage, and code tracking for sampling and remixes.

Release Coordination

Strategic release execution including release date selection and scheduling, pre-save and pre-add campaign setup, coordinated multi-platform launches, territory-specific release strategies, version management (clean, explicit, instrumental), and takedown and redelivery when needed.

Revenue Analytics

Comprehensive reporting on your distribution performance: real-time dashboard access, streaming and download numbers by platform, revenue breakdown by territory and platform, trend analysis and comparative performance, playlist and algorithmic radio tracking, and fan demographic data.
