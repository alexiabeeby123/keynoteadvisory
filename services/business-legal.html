Keynote Advisory provides comprehensive business development and legal advisory services tailored to the music industry. We help artists, producers, labels, and music businesses establish proper corporate structures, develop operational frameworks, and navigate complex legal requirements across jurisdictions. Our services provide the commercial and legal foundation necessary for sustainable growth and asset protection.

Business Development

Entity Formation & Structuring

Proper business structure is critical for liability protection, tax optimization, and professional credibility. We guide you through entity selection (sole proprietorship, partnership, LLC, corporation, or hybrid structures), multi-entity strategies (separate entities for recording, publishing, touring, and merchandising), jurisdiction selection based on tax treatment and legal protections, and foreign entity setup for global operations.

Partnership Agreements

Clear partnership agreements prevent disputes and provide operational clarity: ownership percentages and capital contributions, profit and loss allocation, management authority and decision-making processes, admission and exit procedures, dispute resolution mechanisms, and dissolution and liquidation terms.

Operational Frameworks

Systemized operations that scale with your growth including workflow documentation and standard operating procedures, revenue collection and distribution systems, expense management and approval processes, team roles and reporting structures, quality control and brand standards, and technology infrastructure recommendations.

"Structure today. Scale tomorrow. Succeed forever."

Legal Advisory

Contract Review & Drafting

Comprehensive legal review and drafting of commercial agreements, service provider contracts, licensing agreements, employment and independent contractor agreements, non-disclosure and confidentiality agreements, and terms of service.

Intellectual Property

Safeguarding your creative and commercial assets through trademark registration and enforcement, copyright strategy and protection, trade secret identification, IP portfolio management, and infringement response.

Regulatory Compliance

Navigating complex legal requirements including business licensing and permits, employment law compliance, tax registration and reporting, data protection and privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA), industry-specific regulations, and international trade compliance.

Risk Management

Identifying and mitigating legal and business risks through liability assessment, insurance needs analysis, contract risk review, dispute avoidance strategies, and crisis management planning.
